Open Access Solar: Clean, Affordable Power for Businesses
Open Access Solar is the smart way for businesses to cut energy bills and go green—without the hassle of installing their own solar panels. Start your journey to affordable, renewable power today!
How It Works
  1. Solar Generation: A solar plant generates electricity, either within your state or nearby.
  2. Power Purchase Agreement (PPA): You sign a long-term contract (10–25 years) with the developer at a stable, competitive rate—often much lower than local utility tariffs.
  3. Grid Injection: The solar power is fed into the state’s transmission and distribution network.
  4. Delivery: The grid delivers the power to your facility using existing infrastructure.
  5. Billing & Credits: You receive credits for the solar energy used on your electricity bill and pay only the required grid charges.
Why Choose Open Access?
  • Lower Energy Costs: Pay ₹3.50–₹5.50/unit instead of ₹9.50–₹11.00/unit (varies by state).
  • Stable Pricing: Lock in your energy rates for years, protecting against future price hikes.
  • No Upfront Investment: No need for capital expenditure—just pay for the power you use.
  • Green Credentials: Reduce your carbon footprint and meet sustainability or ESG goals.
  • Flexible Sourcing: Choose what percentage of your energy comes from open access—25%, 50%, or even 100%.
Best For
  • Manufacturing plants
  • Data centers
  • Warehouses & logistics parks
  • Commercial complexes & malls
  • Large institutional campuses
Summary: Open Access Solar is the smart way for businesses to cut energy bills and go green—without the hassle of installing their own solar panels. Start your journey to affordable, renewable power today!

EPC & I&C Services for Renewable Energy Projects
EPC (Engineering, Procurement & Construction) is a turnkey solution where we handle everything from design and procurement to construction and delivery of your renewable energy project—ensuring on-time completion, quality, and performance guarantees.

What is I&C?
I&C (Installation & Commissioning) covers the expert installation, testing, and grid synchronization of your solar or wind system—ideal if you already have design and procurement handled but need a reliable technical partner for on-site execution.

Our EPC Scope of Work

  • Procurement: Sourcing Tier 1 modules, inverters, transformers, cables, and logistics.

Asset management means end-to-end supervision and optimization of solar, wind, or hybrid power projects throughout their lifecycle. It ensures your assets run efficiently, stay compliant, and deliver the best returns—crucial for investors, IPPs, and developers seeking consistent generation and long-term profitability.
Scope of Asset Management Services

  • O&M Supervision: Oversight of maintenance contractors, preventive/corrective schedules, and performance benchmarking.

Financing for Renewable Energy Projects
The right financing model makes renewable energy projects accessible and profitable—reducing upfront costs, speeding up ROI, and unlocking scale. We offer end-to-end financial solutions tailored to your project and regulatory needs.
Financing Models We Support
  • CAPEX Model: 100% upfront investment, full asset ownership, maximum savings—best for those with low-cost capital.
  • OPEX / RESCO Model: No upfront cost, pay per kWh, asset owned and operated by developer—ideal for asset-light industries.
  • Group Captive Model: Consumer co-owns plant, gets cross-subsidy waiver, mix of equity and debt—popular for open access projects.
  • Lease Financing: Monthly rentals, option to buy after lease, off-balance sheet—flexible for long-term users.
  • Bank Loan & NBFC Funding: Term loans at competitive rates, project finance, working capital, and bridge loans.
Our Financing Support Includes
  • DPR and financial modeling
  • PPA structuring and revenue estimation
  • Liaison with banks, NBFCs, investors, and leasing partners
  • End-to-end documentation and sanction support
  • Loan tie-up with PSU and private sector banks
Why Financing Matters
  • Reduces burden of high capital cost
  • Unlocks scale for large utility & open access projects
  • Increases investor confidence
  • Faster project execution with better cash flow planning
